Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1 cup of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup of grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to a thickness of about 1/4 inch.
  2. Place the dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread crushed tomatoes over the dough, leaving a 1/2-inch border around the edge.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the tomatoes.
  5. Drizzle olive oil over the cheese.
  6. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and oregano over the pizza.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
  9. Slice and serve.
