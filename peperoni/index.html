Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 cup of grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of sliced pepperoni
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to a thickness of about 1/4 inch.
  2. Place the dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a 1/2-inch border around the edge.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add pepperoni slices on top of cheese.
  6. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza.
  7. Sprinkle oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper over the pizza.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
