Meatball Sub

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4-6 sub rolls
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, parsley, minced garlic, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp pepper. Mix well and form into small meatballs.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and minced garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Add meatballs to the skillet and cook for 15-20 minutes, or until cooked through.
  5. Slice sub rolls in half and toast if desired.
  6. Spoon meatballs and sauce onto the rolls and top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  7. Bake in the oven at 350Â°F for 5-10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your Meatball Sub!
