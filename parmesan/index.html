Chicken Parmesan Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 4-6 sub rolls

Instructions:

  1. Pound chicken breasts to an even thickness.
  2. Set up a breading station with three shallow dishes: one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dredge each chicken breast in flour, then dip in beaten eggs, and coat in breadcrumb mixture.
  4. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add chicken breasts and cook for 4-5 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Remove chicken from skillet and top with marinara sauce and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Return to the skillet and cook until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Slice sub rolls in half and toast if desired.
  8. Place chicken on the rolls and serve immediately.
