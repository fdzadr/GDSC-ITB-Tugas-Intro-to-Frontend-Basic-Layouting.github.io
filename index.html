About dePizza

At dePizza, we have been serving delicious, hand-tossed pizzas for over 20 years. Our dough is made fresh daily, and we use only the freshest ingredients to make our sauces and toppings. Our menu features a wide variety of classic and specialty pizzas, as well as salads, sandwiches, and desserts. We also offer gluten-free and vegan options. Our cozy, family-friendly atmosphere is perfect for a casual dinner or a special occasion. We also offer online ordering and delivery services for your convenience. We are committed to providing our customers with the best pizza and dining experience possible. Thank you for choosing dePizza.
