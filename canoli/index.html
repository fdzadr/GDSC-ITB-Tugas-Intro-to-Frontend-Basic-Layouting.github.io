Cannoli

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, chilled and diced
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tbsp cold water
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup chopped chocolate or mini chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, cinnamon, and salt.
  2. Add the butter and using your fingers, work the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Whisk the egg yolk and cold water together in a small bowl and add to the flour mixture. Mix until the dough comes together.
  4. Form the dough into a disk and wrap in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface to 1/8 inch thickness. Cut the dough into 3-4 inch circles.
  6. Gently press the dough circles into cannoli molds or roll them around the handle of a wooden spoon.
  7. Heat the vegetable oil in a deep saucepan over medium heat. Carefully add the cannoli shells and fry for 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown and crisp.
  8. Remove the cannoli shells from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  9. In a medium mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, confectioners' sugar, vanilla extract, and chopped chocolate or chocolate chips.
  10. Spoon the ricotta filling into a pastry bag fitted with a large round tip and pipe the filling into the cooled cannoli shells.
  11. Serve immediately or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days.
