Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/4 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup strong coffee
  • 1/4 cup coffee liqueur (such as Kahlua or Marsala)
  • 1 package ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder, for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat egg yolks and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  2. Add mascarpone cheese to the egg mixture and mix until smooth.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  4. Fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture.
  5. Mix coffee and coffee liqueur together in a shallow dish.
  6. Dip ladyfingers into the coffee mixture quickly (do not soak), and layer them in the bottom of an 8x8 inch dish.
  7. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  8. Repeat layering with ladyfingers, coffee mixture, and mascarpone mixture.
  9. Dust the top with cocoa powder.
  10. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight, before serving.
